1. What is C-Score?

Welcome to the C-Score Help Center, your go-to resource for all queries related to understanding and using the C-Score platform. Explore our guides, FAQs, and troubleshooting tips to make the most of your experience.

2. Getting Started with C-Score?

a. Account Setup

  • Free Account: Designed for individuals and small businesses, the free account provides basic compliance tracking, penalty alerts, and filing reminders.

  • Paid Account: Ideal for companies and corporates, offering full access to vendor compliance statuses, tax default checks, and real-time monitoring.

a. Account Setup

  • Visit C-Score.com and click "Sign Up".
  • Choose between Free and Paid plans.
  • Fill in your personal or company details.
  • Verify your email and start tracking compliance!

3. How Does C-Score Work?

  • Tracking: Monitor your personal or business compliance status, including GST returns, TDS filings, and other regulatory requirements.
  • Vendor & Buyer Compliance: Track your vendor’s GST payments, TDS defaults, and compliance history in real time.
  • C-Score: Receive a compliance score that reflects your regulatory health, helping you mitigate risks and plan ahead.

4. C-Score Features Explained

  • Compliance Status: Get real-time updates on your filing statuses and penalties, along with notifications for upcoming deadlines.
  • Penalty Calculations: Receive instant calculations on potential fines for late filings, helping you act proactively.
  • Vendor Monitoring: Keep an eye on your vendors to ensure their compliance, reducing tax and legal risks for your business.
  • Notifications & Alerts: Stay updated with alerts for regulatory changes and deadlines to avoid missing important filings.
  • Virtual Assistance: Use step-by-step guidance from our virtual accountant to rectify compliance issues.

5. Understanding Your C-Score

Your C-Score is a numerical representation of your compliance health. It is calculated based on timely filings, accuracy of information, and adherence to regulations. A higher score indicates better compliance, helping businesses secure trust from partners and avoid legal risks.

  • Improving Your C-Score: To improve your score, ensure that all filings are done on time, rectify any non-compliance issues promptly, and monitor your vendors' statuses.

6. Common FAQs

1. What happens if my C-Score is low?

A low C-Score indicates potential compliance risks. This could result in financial penalties or loss of business credibility. Use the platform’s guidance to resolve pending issues and improve your score.

2. How often is my compliance status updated?

Your status is updated in real-time, ensuring you always have the latest information on your compliance.

3. Can I add multiple vendors to monitor?

Yes, paid users can add and monitor multiple vendors and buyers to ensure their compliance statuses are up-to-date.

7. Troubleshooting

1. I’m not receiving notifications for deadlines.

Ensure that notifications are enabled in your account settings. If the issue persists, check your email’s spam folder or contact our support team.

2. My vendor’s compliance status isn't updated.

The system updates vendor statuses based on their filings. Please allow 24-48 hours after filing for the updates to reflect.
